  • Commands
  • /play - Play music from the given search query.
  • /queue - Display the queue of the musics.
  • /stop - Stop the player and clear the playlist.
  • /pause - Pause the player.
  • /resume - Resume the player.
  • /connect - Make Musicord join your current voice channel.
  • /disconnect - Make Musicord leave the current voice channel.
  • /invite - Generate an invite for Musicord.
